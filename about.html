About

BRG Trainings is a training platform created by Budh Ram Gurung to provide quality trainings on Programming and Software Development.

He preferred to be called as BRG. And, hence named this platform as "BRG Trainings". BRG is a Teacher, Educator, EdTechpreneur, Open Source Contributor, Software Engineer and Researcher.

He switched to full time training to follow his passion of teaching. He has an overall 8.5+ years of IT industry experience and nearly worked in all stack of Software Development. He is an Open-Source Advocate and do coding for fun.

Learning Approach

Self-Learning

(Learn by Yourself)

Most of the syllabus in this platform is designed in such a way that you can go through it and learn by yourself.

  • Completely Free
  • Community Support
  • Slow Learning Approach
  • Less Teacher Attention

Paid Training

(Learn with BRG)

In the paid training, BRG will be teaching using this platform and help you gain valued knowledge.

  • Teacher Led Training
  • Valued Knowledge
  • Fast Learning Approach
  • Focussed Teacher Attention
  • Assignment and Feedback Oriented
